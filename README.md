# Catalyst


Catalyst is an innovative social welfare platform that empowers individuals and communities to work together toward a cleaner, safer, and more sustainable future. By combining cleanliness initiatives, data-driven monitoring, and community engagement, Catalyst promotes meaningful participation and responsible action. Its unique three-stage reward system recognizes contributions and encourages continuous improvement, fostering accountability and collaboration. Through technology and collective effort, Catalyst transforms community participation into measurable impact, creating an environment where everyone can contribute to building a better society.
